Spawning

Naturally spawning Gronckles appear in the Overworld. Those within the "Other" category will be explained in a later update. Typically, they appear in groups of a size that ranges from 2 to 5, but sometimes a female Gronckle is able to spawn together with a baby, increasing the size of the group.

The chances for a Female Gronckle to spawn with a baby near her are 1% (1/100). This baby will have the same color as the mother, although the stats might differ.

The gender and variant of wild Gronckles will always be random.

Gronckles will also spawn if you use a Gronckle spawn egg on any block or entity. When the spawn egg is used on a Gronckle, it will spawn a baby.

When a Gronckle spawns, each stat will be randomly generated within a certain range.
The ranges for each stat are as follows:

Stats Range
Health 50-70
Armor 35-45
Damage 6-10
Walkspeed 0.3-0.5
FlySpeed 0.3-0.5

Behaviour

Gronckles are peaceful dragons that will not attack the player.

When holding 1 of the items used for taming a Gronckle, it will follow the player around.
If the player is absent or not holding taming items and the Gronckle is not tamed, it will wander around aimlessly.

After taming a Gronckle, right-clicking on it with an empty hand will let you ride it.
You can also let it follow you by right-clicking on it with Valka's Staff or by pressing the "All Follow" keybind (G by default).
If you right-click a Gronckle with a Gronckle Saddle, the dragon will equip it. However, as of Alpha 0.0.7 the Gronckle Saddle is completely cosmetic.

Taming & Breeding

In order to tame a Gronckle, you are required to hold one of the following items and right-click on one in the wild.
In Survival Mode, the taming success rate is 30% for each item.
In Creative Mode, the taming is guaranteed.

The items that can be used to tame a Gronckle are:

Stone
Cobblestone
Deepslate
Cobbled Deepslate
Sandstone
Andesite
Diorite
Granite
Raw Cod
Raw Salmon

In order to breed Gronckles, Right-click a Male and Female with any of the breeding items in your hand.
Note that you can only breed Gronckles if the weather is clear.

The items that can be used to breed Gronckles are:

Stone
Cobblestone
Deepslate
Cobbled Deepslate
Sandstone
Andesite
Diorite
Granite

After Breeding 2 Gronckles, the female will lay an egg.
After the egg is laid, the female will receive a "Breeding Cooldown" of 10 minutes seconds before it is able to breed again.
The male will not receive a cooldown.

To find out how to hatch a Gronckle Egg, check out the Incubator page.

When a Gronckle egg hatches, a Baby Gronckle spawns. For more information on how the inheritance system works, check out "Genetics"

Genetics

When a Gronckle egg hatches, a Baby Gronckle spawns.
The baby will have a 50/50 chance to inherit a color and gender from either parent.
This same chance applies for inheriting stats from it's parents.

The stats that can be inherited are:

Health
Armor
Damage
Walkspeed
Flyspeed
Swimspeed

There is a 1% chance that the stat of the Baby gets mutated, increasing the stat by 1 Point.
The Gronckle can have a maximum of 50 mutations, with a maximum of 20 mutations per stat.

Abilities

The Gronckle has as, of alpha 0.0.7, only two abilities, which are Fireball and Gronckle Iron creation.

By pressing the ability keybind (R by default), the Gronckle is able to fire a Fireball from its mouth.
The Gronckle has a Firing Capacity of 6 Fireballs by default. After shooting the First Fireball, the Gronckle starts to recharge its Fireball Capacity until it is fully recharged.
The recharge time is 30 seconds per Fireball.

On impact, the Fireball will explode and deal 5 damage to any entity within a 2 block radius.
