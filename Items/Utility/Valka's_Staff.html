Valka's Staff

Valka's Staff  is an item used to make specific dragons follow the player around. This page contains every single bit of information there is to know about the Item in game. The subjects covered by this page are:

Valka's Staff

Information
Item type: Utility
Canon use: Used by Valka to command her dragons
Use in the mod: To make specific dragons follow the player

Usage

With the Tier 1 Incubator, you can hatch all eggs at a speed per egg (which depends on what egg it is), and it has certain requirements for each type of dragon egg in order to complete the hatching process for that egg.

Requirements for Hatching
Dragon Egg Requirement Time to hatch
Gronckle Lava Bucket 20 minutes
Deadly Nadder Flint 20 minutes
Terrible Terror Rabbit's Foot 5 minutes
